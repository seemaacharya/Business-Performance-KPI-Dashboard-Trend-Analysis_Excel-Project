### Business Performance KPI Dashboard & Trend Analysis – Excel Project

This repository presents a Business Analyst engagement focused on transforming raw sales data into a structured KPI reporting framework to support data-driven decision-making.

## Business Context

The organization lacked clear KPI visibility, relied on manual Excel reporting, and had limited insight into product performance and customer buying behavior.

## My Role

Defined business KPIs (Revenue, Orders, AOV, Trends)

Cleaned and validated transactional data

Built an interactive dashboard using Pivot Tables & Slicers

Analyzed SKU performance and time-based sales patterns

Delivered actionable business recommendations

## Tools Used

Advanced Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
Slicers & KPI Dashboard Design
Data Cleaning & Validation Techniques

## Outcome

Developed Excel dashboard on 40,000+ records, improving revenue visibility

Automated reporting processes, reducing manual effort by ~40%

Improved KPI visibility by ~60–70% for leadership through automated dashboards and structured performance reporting

Identified top-performing SKUs contributing to 15–20% of total revenue

Improved reporting turnaround time by ~30%
